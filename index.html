Maxinejiji

Maxinejiji or Maxine Lat Calibuso in real life is a Filipino
writer who wrote the famous Wattpad series "He's Into Her".
Her works are published under Life is Beautiful
(LIB Creatives)/Pastrybug which is also a part of Precious Pages Corporation.

Three Best Stories

Maxpein del Valle, a spunky provincial girl raised by her grandmother and uncle after her mother dies, moves to Manila with her newly discovered wealthy father where she is treated as an unwelcome outsider at Benison International School and bullied by Deib Lohr Enrile, the basketball varsity captain.

He's Into Her

The inspiring and romantic story of how bestselling author and motivational speaker Nick Vujicic met and married his soulmate, Kanae. It doesn't take a perfect person to find a perfect love. Even though he was born without arms or legs, Nick Vujicic created a 'ridiculously good life' for himself.

Love Without Limits

Wishful Thinking is the story of obsession and the lust in the mist of so called „love“. How far will one go to find out the extent of love? When reality presses in, Catherine discovers the darkness in her love. As she stands in front of disillusion, she gives birth to temptation and succumbs herself.

Why Maxinejiji?

Admiring Maxinejiji as a Wattpad story writer is a personal celebration of her ability to create stories that connect on a deep level. It's about valuing storytelling as a collaborative experience, where readers and writers share a journey. Idolizing Maxinejiji is also embracing the democratization of literature, seeing her success as a testament to the power of individual voices on global platforms. In essence, it's a nod to the transformative impact of digital storytelling and the inspiration it provides for aspiring writers
